The Hyrule Compendium is a feature found in Breath of the Wild . It lists various Creatures, Monsters, Materials, Equipment and Treasure encountered by Link during his travels through the various regions of Hyrule .

Information is added to the compendium by taking pictures with the Sheikah Slate of various items once the Camera Rune has been unlocked. Most pictures can also be bought in Hateno Ancient Tech Lab for 100 Rupees each from Symin , after the Sunshroom Sensing side-quest has been completed. Once added to the compendium, any item can be set to the Sheikah Sensor+, allowing Link to track its location.

Please note that the numbering on the transcribed list below includes the four entries added in the second DLC pack, The Champions' Ballad . Your numbering may vary, depending on the version, or if you are playing in Master Mode. For the full numbering, see Gallery:Breath of the Wild Hyrule Compendium .

Notes

  • If no picture was taken of the Master Sword before obtaining it from the Korok Forest , as the obtained sword can't be dropped to take a picture from a distance, a picture can be taken by making a self-portrait while having the sword equipped on its own without shields or bows. The self-portrait feature of the Camera Rune also allows detection of the sword.
  • When the Hyrule Compendium is completed, Symin gives Link a picture depicting a beautiful woman of the Sheikah Tribe, which is implied to be Purah in her youth. It wouldn't be until Age of Calamity that it's shown that Purah was indeed remarkably beautiful.
  • Weapons, shields and bows obtainable through amiibo do not have entries in the Hyrule Compendium.
  • The entries in the Hyrule Compendium cannot be used on any quest that involves pictures to be taken with the Camera Rune . Said quests require the pictures to be in the album accessible through the (-) menu. For instance, if a picture of a Guardian Stalker is required for the Guardian Slideshow quest, the entry in the Hyrule Compendium alone is not enough to fulfill the requirement regardless of it having an image of the Stalker; hence, a picture has to be taken.
